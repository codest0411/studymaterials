Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q1: How can I access the study materials?

A1: You can access the study materials by navigating to the "Study Materials" section from the main menu and clicking on the relevant links.

Q2: How do I change my password?

A2: To change your password, go to the "Settings" page, enter your new password in the "Change Password" section, and click "Save Changes".

Technical Support

Q3: What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

A3: If you encounter any technical issues, please contact our support team at support@example.com.

Q4: How do I change the dashboard language?

A4: To change the dashboard language, go to the "Settings" page, select your preferred language from the "Dashboard Language" dropdown, and click "Save Changes".
